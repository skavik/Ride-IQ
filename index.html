OPPORTUNITY

Passengers will take their rides IN CONTROL. Enter your location and destination place your “wish” price for the ride. We will make your “wish” price for the trip available to the network of providers.

Drivers will take their business to the highest level nearby cars or cars would presumably quote more aggressive prices, resulting in the user securing the best cost for his trip.

Many fleets have one way trips and their cars return empty, this approach will give them a chance to bid on offers that make sense. Bidding will be available for a short time (10-15 minutes). After bidding is “closed”, users will be still presented with an option of choosing from all companies based on $ and ETA for the desired destination.

OPTION

The idea of this platform is to create a customer App that will give people a choice of getting a ride from many competitors based on $ vs ETA. This platform also will give the customer an option to open bid for their trip. For example:

" I’m at AIRPORT and need to go to DOWNTOWN ; I select my FROM and TO and enter the price I’m willing to spend for that trip.

App will show a suggested price based on lowest historical average for that route. Let’s say suggested price is $55, but I’m entering my “wish price” of $35. This gives companies a chance to bid on this offer if they have available cars at the same destination.

WHO WE ARE

As is often the case, the genesis of MoveAround Pro (M.A.P.) ( www.movearoundpro.com ) was inspired by the recognition of an unfilled marketplace need which, in the course of their industry experiences, was identified by the founders. That need - the development of a fully mobile, cloud based solution for app based car fleets - was the seed that gave birth to M.A.P.

The company, with offices in NC and NY today, is a young software provider with a unique product concept ...

a suite of software tools to monitor and control on-demand ride booking and sharing and car flee operations

At the dispatcher, driver and customer levels but with fully portable functionality and delivering a streamlined operating experience.
